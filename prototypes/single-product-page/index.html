Gate valves for industrial service in Bangladesh

A gate valve is the default isolation valve for full-bore, low-pressure-drop shut-off on water, steam, oil, and general process lines. Cast carbon-steel (WCB) gate valves in DN100 PN16 are among the most specified line sizes in Bangladeshi industry — from textile dye houses and power utilities to food processing and pharmaceutical plants.

When selecting a DN100 gate valve, procurement teams typically verify the body and trim materials (A216 WCB with 13% Cr trim is the common baseline), the face-to-face standard (EN 558-1 / ASME B16.10), flange drilling (EN 1092-1 PN16), and pressure–temperature ratings for the intended service. Hydrostatic testing to EN 12266-1 or API 598 with mill test certificates is standard for plant acceptance.
